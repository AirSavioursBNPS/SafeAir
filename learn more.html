What is Air Pollution?

Air Pollution

Air pollution occurs when harmful substances like particulates and gases contaminate the air. It impacts both human health and the environment, posing a global challenge.

Causes of Air Pollution

  • Burning of fossil fuels for energy and transportation.
  • Industrial emissions releasing harmful pollutants.
  • Wildfires and agricultural residue burning.
  • Excessive vehicle emissions in urban areas.
Impacts of Air Pollution

On Health

Causes respiratory issues, cardiovascular diseases, and shortens life expectancy. Children and the elderly are highly vulnerable.

On Environment

Damages ecosystems, reduces crop yield, contributes to acid rain, and accelerates global warming.

Solutions

  • Shift to renewable energy and reduce fossil fuel dependence.
  • Plant more trees to enhance air quality.
  • Encourage public transport and carpooling.
